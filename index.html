About Us!

GF has brought together more than 100 products from East Asian food under one roof, offering you a smile and healthy food for you and your children.

Our objectives and goals are to ensure customer satisfaction and happiness, great personal service providing high quality of food and selling at affordable price to all customers in order to promote the restaurant concept as a unique restaurant that create a healthy and safe environment.

Our Expects Chef

At GF Eat Street Restaurant, our expert chef is the driving force behind the extraordinary dining experiences we offer. Their culinary expertise, creative flair, and dedication to quality shine through in every dish served.

Join us and savor the flavors that our chef expertly crafts, as we invite you to indulge in an unforgettable culinary journey.
